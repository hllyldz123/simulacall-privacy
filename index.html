Last updated: 11 December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Simulacall application (“App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) handles user data. Simulacall is a fake call simulation app designed for entertainment purposes.

Simulacall does NOT collect, store, sell, or share any personal or sensitive data. We do not collect:

  • Personal information

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party analytics, tracking, ads, or SDKs.

5. Children’s Privacy

The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.
